FOUNDER- GIRLSCRIPT FOUNDATION

I started GirlScript as a project when I was in final year of my Engineering. I noticed various flaws like lack of skills, students buying projects, less women participation in technical events and lack of awareness. At Present, GirlScript is a registered non-profit organisation working in more than 4 cities of India and rapidly expanding!

EXPERT MENTOR- MOZILLA OPEN LEADERS

I was a mentee in Open Leaders Cohort-3 and after that I got a chance to become a mentor at Cohort-4 of Open Leadership Program.

COACH- LEARN IT, GIRL

MOZILLA REPRESENTATIVE- REPS

I joined Mozilla as a Firefox student ambassador, then I started my own Mozilla Club to promote web litercy. I represented the organisation at Maker Fest 2017 and after some significant contributions, I became part of Mozilla Reps Program.

DJANGO GIRLS OUAGADOUGOU (AFRICA) &  NAGPUR (INDIA)
